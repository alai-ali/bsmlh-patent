Privacy Policy

BSMLH Platform | ALAI CORPORATION, TOO | Effective: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

BSMLH ("we", "our", "the Platform") is operated by ALAI CORPORATION, TOO. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services: identity document data (name, date of birth, document number, nationality) for identity verification purposes, biometric data scanned from your passport chip via NFC technology, and device information necessary for app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to verify your identity and generate your unique digital identifier (HUID). We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. All cryptographic operations are performed locally on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your private keys and sensitive data are stored exclusively on your device using military-grade AES-256-GCM encryption. We do not store your private keys or passport data on our servers. Your identity data never leaves your device in unencrypted form.

5. NFC and Biometric Data

Our application uses NFC technology to read the chip embedded in your biometric passport. This data is used only for identity verification and is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit raw biometric data to any server.

6. Data Retention

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the application settings. Upon deletion, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@bsmlh.app
